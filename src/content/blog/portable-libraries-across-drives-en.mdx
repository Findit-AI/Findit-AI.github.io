Video teams rarely keep footage on one machine.  
Drives move, projects move, and people move.

Search context should move with that reality.

## Why portability matters

If every migration forces full re-indexing, teams lose momentum and confidence.  
Portable library design keeps your retrieval context durable across setups.

## Recommended migration checklist

Before moving drives:

- confirm indexing has completed for active folders
- keep folder hierarchy unchanged during transfer
- document destination paths for collaborators

After connecting on another machine:

- verify mounted paths
- run quick validation queries
- only rebuild affected indexes if needed

## Operational payoff

Portability reduces setup churn, speeds onboarding, and keeps multi-device collaboration predictable.
