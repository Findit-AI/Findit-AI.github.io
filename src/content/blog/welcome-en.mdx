FinDIT is built for teams that already have too much footage and too little time.

Our focus is straightforward: make real media libraries searchable at working speed, without forcing teams to upload everything before they can start.

On this blog, we'll share product updates, workflow guidance, and practical notes from building retrieval infrastructure for real editing pipelines.
