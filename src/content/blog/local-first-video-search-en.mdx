For most video teams, retrieval is the bottleneck.

FinDIT is local-first because baseline search must stay usable under real production conditions: unstable networks, large external drives, and tight delivery windows.

## What local-first means in daily work

- Core indexing and retrieval run on your Mac.
- You do not need to upload the full library to make search useful.
- Drive migration keeps library continuity instead of forcing full restart.

## Where cloud enhancement belongs

Cloud enhancement is valuable for harder semantic queries, but it should improve the baseline, not replace it.

That boundary keeps the product reliable, predictable, and low-risk to adopt.
