FinDIT pricing is intentionally simple: Free and Pro.

## What stays in Free

Free keeps the local-first baseline:

- local indexing
- core search workflow
- day-to-day clip discovery

## What Pro adds

Pro is for teams running heavier semantic workloads:

- higher monthly cloud enhancement budget
- stronger consistency on difficult natural-language queries
- more headroom for production-scale usage

The rule is straightforward: core usefulness stays in Free, while Pro improves hard-query performance at scale.
