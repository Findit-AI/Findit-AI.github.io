Pricing should be clear and concrete.

## What Free and Pro have in common

Core retrieval remains available across the product:

- local indexing
- core search workflow
- day-to-day clip discovery

## What Trial is

Trial is a time-limited evaluation window for cloud semantic enhancement.  
It is used to test difficult queries on your own footage before moving to Pro.

## What Pro adds

Pro is for teams with frequent semantic workloads that need higher monthly cloud budget and steadier quality on hard prompts.
