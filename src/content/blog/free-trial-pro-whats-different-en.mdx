Pricing should be clear and concrete.

## What stays the same

Core retrieval remains available in all plans:

- local indexing
- core search workflow
- day-to-day clip discovery

## What Trial adds

Trial includes time-limited cloud semantic enhancement, so teams can test difficult queries with their own footage.

## What Pro adds

Pro is for teams with frequent semantic workloads that need higher monthly cloud budget and steadier quality on hard prompts.
